Imperial / Northern Faction APC

The Armored Personnel Carrier is an armored vehicle which ferries troops across the battlefield. This vehicle relies primarily on armor plating for its defense as its light armament of grenade launchers and a machine gun is ill-suited to tasks other than clearing an area of infantry. It also acts as a mobile spawnpoint.
